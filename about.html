About CLEAR

Advancing language equity and linguistic sovereignty in the age of artificial intelligence

Our Mission

The Coalition for Language Equity in AI Research (CLEAR) is dedicated to ensuring that the world's linguistic diversity thrives in the age of artificial intelligence.

We bring together researchers, technologists, communities, and institutions to build benchmarks, resources, and frameworks that hold AI systems accountable to the full spectrum of human language. Our work spans from technical evaluation to policy advocacy, always centering the voices and agency of speaker communities.

We believe that language is not merely a communication tool—it is the载体 of culture, identity, and knowledge. When AI systems fail to support a language, they exclude entire communities from the digital future.

Our Vision

Linguistic Sovereignty

Communities have the right to control how their languages are represented, resourced, and deployed in AI systems. We advocate for data governance frameworks that prioritize community consent and benefit.

Equitable benchmarks

Evaluation frameworks that measure not just accuracy, but cultural competence, pragmatic adequacy, and community trust. Our benchmarks are co-designed with speakers of underrepresented languages.

Global Collaboration

Cross-sector partnerships uniting academia, industry, and communities. We believe sustainable progress requires diverse perspectives and shared commitment to long-term impact.

Open Resources

Freely accessible datasets, tools, and playbooks that empower researchers and communities worldwide. We champion open science while respecting indigenous data sovereignty.

Core Values

01

Community-Centered Design

We center the needs, perspectives, and agency of language communities in all our work. Technology should serve people, not the other way around.

02

Transparency & Accountability

We publish our methodologies, data sources, and evaluation criteria openly. AI developers must be accountable for the linguistic gaps in their systems.

03

Intersectional Equity

Language equity intersects with racial, economic, and geographic justice. We address these connections explicitly in our research and advocacy.

04

Long-Term Commitment

Language preservation and AI development are marathon endeavors. We commit to sustained engagement beyond short-term research cycles.
