About CLEAR

The Coalition for Language Equity in AI Research is an interdisciplinary initiative founded in 2026 by leading institutions including LILT, Stanford, Berkeley, and UCLA.

We believe that linguistic diversity is not a technical challenge to solve, but a human right to protect. Our mission is to advance linguistic sovereignty—ensuring communities have agency over how their languages are represented, resourced, and deployed in AI systems.

Through cross-sector collaboration, we're building the infrastructure, benchmarks, and playbooks needed to make AI truly multilingual.

Three Pillars

Benchmarking

Developing rigorous, culturally-grounded evaluation frameworks that measure AI performance across languages—not just accuracy, but cultural competence, pragmatic adequacy, and community trust.

  • Cross-lingual evaluation suites
  • Community-driven annotation protocols
  • Dynamic difficulty adaptation

Resource Collection

Curating and creating high-quality datasets, tools, and infrastructure for low-resource languages. We prioritize data sovereignty— ensuring communities retain control over their linguistic assets.

  • Open dataset repository
  • Ethical data governance frameworks
  • Community annotation toolkits

Strategic Playbooks

Translating research into action with practical guides for researchers, companies, and policymakers. From model training to deployment, we provide evidence-based roadmaps for equitable AI.

  • Industry best practices
  • Policy recommendations
  • Community partnership models
